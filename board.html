Sanjay

Presently working as Professor and Principal, with Aditya Global Business School, Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh. Qualified UGC NET-2011 in Management and earned Ph.D. in Business Management from Kakatiya University, Telangana in 2020. Gained ten years of industry experience in sales and marketing domain, before embarking on an academic career. In industry worked at various positions with India’s well known media houses like Indian Express Newspapers (Bombay) Limited, India Today Group, DNA Newspaper and Times Group (timesjobs.com). At ISB, assisted outstanding faculty not only from ISB but also from world’s best B-schools like Stanford University, The Wharton, Columbia Business School, UCLA etc. In 2012 Credited Entrepreneurship course at ISB. Delivered guest lectures, induction programs to various graduate, post-graduate students in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh states. Participated and presented papers in National and International conferences. Published journal papers listed in Scopus, ABDC and UGC care, cited in sagepub.com, researchgate.net, scitation.org, emraldinsight.com, ieee.org etc. Peer Reviewer for ‘Asia Pacific Journal of Education’ published by Taylor & Francis. This Journal is indexed in Australian Education Index, Australian Public Affairs Information Service (APAIS), Bibliography of Asian Studies, Content Pages in Education, EBSCO (Education Research Complete), Educational Research Abstracts online (ERA), ERIC, PsycINFO, Scopus, Social Sciences Citation Index®, VOCEDPlus. Delivered ‘master class on marketing’ to IIFT Kakinada students. Invited as visiting faculty to Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM), Hyderabad, Vignan University, Guntur, Siva Sivani Institute of Management (SSIM), Hyderabad and Annapurna College of Film & Media (ACFM), Hyderabad. Areas of interest are Sales Management, Marketing Management, Advertising, Branding, Education, Media and Entertainment. Playing key role in academics, admissions, training and placements.
ACADEMIC EXPERIENCE Professor of Marketing & Principal - Aditya Global Business School (07th March 2022 to till date) ➢ Heading three programs, BBA affiliated to Adikavi Nannaya University, IMBA & MBA (Autonomous) affiliated to JNTU-Kakinada with 1000-1200 students’ strength. Presently managing around fifty member team of teaching and non-teaching staff.
➢ Overall monitoring of academic and non-academic activities of the institute which include admissions, placements, pass percentage, discipline, smooth execution of internal exams, handling students-parents grievances, 360o development of the students (Social Outreach, Industrial Visits, Entrepreneurship, Guest Lectures, Value Added Courses, Sports, Cultural and Management Fests etc). Day to day focus on the revenue and expenses of the institute.
➢ Hiring and mentoring of newly hired teaching and non-teaching staff. Measuring and improving teaching effectiveness through student feedback system. Motivating and retaining good faculty. Organizing FDPs for faculty and encouraging them to focus on research activities and career progression. Doing performance appraisal of teaching faculty.
➢ Openness and flexible to innovative ideas and practices for the benefit of the students and institution. Maintaining relationship and transparency with the management, teaching and non teaching staff ensuring and promoting good work culture and ethical practices.

Dr. V Tulasi

Dr. V. Tulasi Das is a distinguished Professor in the Department of Human Resource Management (HRM) at the MBA program. With a comprehensive academic background and over 28 years of teaching and research experience, Dr. Das is a leading figure in the field of Human Resource Management. Dr. Das holds an impressive array of qualifications, including an M.B.A. from University Campus College, Nagarjuna University, a Master of Human Resource Management (M.H.R.M.) from Acharya Nagarjuna University, where she was awarded a Gold Medal, and a Bachelor of Graduate Law (BGL) from Andhra University. Additionally, she has completed a Postgraduate Diploma in Personnel Management (PGDPM) from Annamalai University, an M.Phil. from Andhra University, and a Ph.D. from the same institution. Dr. Das also holds a National Eligibility Test (NET) qualification in Business Management. Her research interests are broad and impactful, encompassing Human Resource Management, Human Resource Development, Strategic Management, and Marketing Management. Dr. Das has supervised the research of 31 Ph.D. candidates and 9 M.Phil. students to completion and is currently guiding 8 Ph.D. scholars. Dr. Das’s scholarly contributions are extensive, with 195 research articles published in national and international journals and 55 book chapters. Her editorial work includes editing 15 books and co-authoring 9. She has actively participated in academic conferences, attending 94 national and 47 international seminars, and presenting 88 and 47 papers respectively. Additionally, she has been involved in organizing 20 national events, including serving as Director for 11 and holding other key roles. A highly regarded resource person, Dr. Das has delivered guest lectures and participated in 49 seminars and conferences. Her academic work has garnered significant recognition, reflected in her Google Scholar citations, which currently total 162. Dr. Das is also listed in the VIDWAN database with ID 111544, underscoring her prominence in the academic community. Through her extensive research, teaching, and organizational roles, Dr. V. Tulasi Das continues to contribute significantly to the advancement of Human Resource Management and related fields.
